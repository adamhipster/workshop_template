What You Learn at The Web Design Workshop

Introduction to the Basics of Web-design

HTML5 and CSS3

Learn about the fundamentals used to create web pages. HTML5 allows you to display the text, images, video and audio you want. CSS3 enables you to place content where you want it and how you want it to look like. Should text be black, purple or animated? You decide through the power of CSS!

JavaScript

Once you know what content you want to place, you need to ask yourself what interaction you want to add to the website. What happens when you click on a button, does a new window appear? Do you want to track how long a user is on your website? All of this is possible with JavaScript.

Your First Digital Product

By following the lectures, coding along and coding on your own you will create your first digital product! The quick introduction shows the relevance of web technologies (HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript) and how they interact with each other.
